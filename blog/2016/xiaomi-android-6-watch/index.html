Solving problems after upgrading Android cwonto Xiaomi

27 Mar 2016 • 1 min. read • Comments •

After upgrading Xiaomi Mi 4LTE to MIUI 7.2.9.0 (MXDCNDB), the version of the android in the phone changed to 6.0.1. As a side effect, the watch LG G Watch R 1085 has disconnected from the phone, and on the phone itself Google Play Services panicked. Below is the way to restore the phone-to-watch connection.

  1. Remove Google apps

  2. Reboot to native Recovery and clear the cache (Wipe Cache).

  3. Setup the Google Installer. It’s easy found in Mi store.

  4. Reboot.

  5. Launch Google Play and install Google App.

  6. Reboot.

  7. Remove Google account.

  8. Reboot.

  9. Launch Google Play and setup Android Wear.
